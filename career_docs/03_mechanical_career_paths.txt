DOCUMENT: Mechanical Engineering - Complete Career Guide

=== CAREER PATHS ===

1. AUTOMOTIVE ENGINEER
Role: Design and develop vehicles, engines, transmission systems, body structures.
Skills Required: AutoCAD, CATIA V5, SolidWorks, ANSYS, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, GD&T.
Top Companies: Tata Motors, Mahindra, Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Bosch Automotive, KPIT Technologies, Ricardo.
Entry Salary: 3-10 LPA.
Career Growth: Design Engineer -> Senior Engineer -> Project Lead -> Chief Engineer.

2. MANUFACTURING / PRODUCTION ENGINEER
Role: Plan and optimize manufacturing processes, manage production lines.
Skills Required: Lean Manufacturing, Six Sigma, AutoCAD, SolidWorks, CNC programming, quality management (ISO standards).
Top Companies: Tata Steel, Larsen & Toubro, Bharat Forge, Hindustan Unilever, Godrej, Kirloskar.
Entry Salary: 3-8 LPA.
Certifications: Six Sigma Green Belt / Black Belt, Lean Manufacturing certification.

3. ROBOTICS AND AUTOMATION ENGINEER
Role: Design automated systems and robots for manufacturing and industry.
Skills Required: PLC programming (Siemens, Allen Bradley), SCADA, AutoCAD Electrical, Python, ROS, robot arm programming.
Top Companies: ABB, Fanuc, Yaskawa, Addverb Technologies, GreyOrange, Wipro Industrial.
Entry Salary: 4-12 LPA.

4. AEROSPACE ENGINEER
Role: Design aircraft, spacecraft, missiles, satellites.
Skills Required: CATIA, ANSYS Fluent, aerodynamics, structural analysis, composites knowledge, MATLAB.
Top Companies: HAL (Hindustan Aeronautics Limited), DRDO, ISRO, Boeing India, Airbus, Safran.
Entry Salary: 4-12 LPA.
Note: GATE exam opens PSU jobs at HAL, DRDO, ISRO with salary 6-12 LPA.

5. THERMAL / HVAC ENGINEER
Role: Design heating, ventilation, air conditioning systems for buildings and industrial use.
Skills Required: Thermodynamics, heat transfer, AutoCAD MEP, HVAC design software (HAP, Carrier), energy auditing.
Top Companies: Johnson Controls, Daikin, Voltas (TATA), Blue Star, Honeywell Building Technologies.
Entry Salary: 3-8 LPA.

6. PRODUCT DESIGN ENGINEER
Role: Design new consumer or industrial products from concept to manufacturing.
Skills Required: SolidWorks, CATIA, Fusion 360, 3D printing, design thinking, rapid prototyping, materials science.
Top Companies: Frog Design, Ideo, Whirlpool, Godrej Appliances, Philips India.
Entry Salary: 4-10 LPA.

7. OIL AND GAS / PETROLEUM ENGINEER
Role: Design and oversee drilling, extraction, and processing of oil and gas.
Skills Required: Process engineering, piping design, AutoCAD P&ID, ASME codes, safety standards, HAZOP.
Top Companies: ONGC, IOCL, BPCL, Shell, ExxonMobil, Reliance Industries, Engineers India Limited.
Entry Salary: 5-15 LPA.

8. STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS ENGINEER
Role: Analyze structures for strength, durability under loads.
Skills Required: ANSYS, Abaqus, NASTRAN, FEA (Finite Element Analysis), SolidWorks Simulation.
Top Companies: L&T, TATA Projects, Jacobs Engineering, WSP Global, AECOM.
Entry Salary: 4-10 LPA.

=== INTERNSHIP OPPORTUNITIES FOR MECHANICAL STUDENTS ===

Government: DRDO, ISRO, HAL, NTPC, BHEL, ONGC, SAIL (Steel Authority of India).
Private: Tata Motors, Mahindra, Bosch, L&T, Bharat Forge.
Duration: Usually 6-8 weeks in summer (May-June) or 6 months in final year.
Stipend: Government 10,000-15,000/month, Private 5,000-25,000/month.

How to Secure Internships:
- Learn at least one CAD software (SolidWorks or CATIA) before applying.
- Do NPTEL courses in Manufacturing or Design.
- Apply through college placement cell for PSU internships.
- Apply on Internshala for private company internships.

=== GATE EXAM FOR MECHANICAL ENGINEERS ===

Why GATE: Mandatory for PSU jobs (BHEL, ONGC, NTPC, DRDO) and M.Tech admissions at IITs/NITs.
GATE ME Syllabus Key Topics: Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Machine Design, Manufacturing, Engineering Mechanics.
Preparation: 6-12 months dedicated preparation. Use Made Easy or GATE Academy material.
PSU Salary After GATE: 6-14 LPA for fresh graduates.

=== SKILL ROADMAP ===

Core: Engineering Drawing, AutoCAD 2D, then 3D CAD (SolidWorks/CATIA).
Analysis: ANSYS for FEA simulation.
Programming (optional but valuable): Python for automation, MATLAB for simulation.
Certifications: SolidWorks CSWA/CSWP certification is industry recognized.
